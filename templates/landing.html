YOLOv5 Landing Assistant

The most important and most terrifying part of a flight is landing. The world has seen many crashes that happened when the flight was a few minutes away from landing, many lives were lost in that. In order to make a small step towards reducing crashes we have designed an object detection model using YOLOv5 which will help the pilot see the runway clearly from a certain distance above the ground itself. Here all we have to do is give the algorithm an image or video file, Go ahead and upload your files and see the magic
